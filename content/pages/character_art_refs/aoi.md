#Important Notes
While her design includes several distinguishing details (such as her freckles), in-universe Aoi is considered unremarkable and plain-looking. Many don't notice her presence unless she makes herself known, and most forget her and what her face looked like quickly.

Aoi doesn't mind being so easily forgotten, especially because it helps her listen in on conversations without being noticed and eventually earned her a job with Solana Administration. She doesn't let herself get attached to most people, but she clings tightly to those who do remember her.
