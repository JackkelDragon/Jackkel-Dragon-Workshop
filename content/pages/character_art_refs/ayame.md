#Important Notes

Ayame is relatively tall, though this contrasts significantly with how she tends to dress and style her hair. She usually wears feminine clothing, and her hairstyle can make her look younger than she is. These incongruities in her appearance can cause her to seem odd to those not familiar with her.

A formal and reserved woman most of the time, Ayame usually comes across as oddly polite when she interacts with people. Under this facade she hides her powerful emotions, which can manifest as an intense passion when she feels comfortable in a situation.
