#Important Notes

Hoshiko is cheerful and fun-loving, almost naive in some ways. While she once went with the flow of her peers, a sudden revelation about someone she had trusted has caused her to commit to her own path. While she's not always very talented at the things she wants to do, she puts in extra effort in order to be there for her friends.
