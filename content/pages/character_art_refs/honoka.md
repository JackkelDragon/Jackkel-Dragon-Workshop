#Important Notes
Honoka has a similar appearance to her younger sister (Kagami), but usually keeps her hair much shorter and neater.

Honoka has an outgoing and playful personality, contrasting strongly with her little sister. While she seems to excel in understanding the feelings of other extroverts, she has a bit of a blind spot with atypical people like her sister, leading her to inadvertantly step over the feelings of such people. As an adult, she works to fill in the gaps in her ability to empathize in order to be a more supportive friend and sister.
